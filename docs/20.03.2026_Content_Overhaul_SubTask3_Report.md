## Objective

Create a dedicated 50-item vocabulary pack of English–French false cognates ("false friends") with 100% `frenchTrap` coverage. Each item highlights a word that looks similar in French and English but has a different meaning.

---

## What Was Done

### Step 1: Created `src/data/vocabularyPacks/false-friends.ts`

A new vocabulary pack with 50 items (ff-001 through ff-050), organized by CEFR level:

**A2 Level (15 items):** actually, attend, library, coin, chair, figure, magazine, sympathetic, sensible, rest, preservative, surname, grape, fabric, location

**B1 Level (15 items):** eventually, engage, resume, affair, petrol, conductor, introduce, phrase, comprehensive, confident, abuse, hazard, novel, supply, patron

**B2 Level (12 items):** deception, injury, delay, advertisement, commodity, physician, lecture, journey, intoxicated, formidable, caution, trivial

**C1 Level (8 items):** fastidious, petulant, ostensible, affluent, achieve, bless, candid, envy
