**Calibration-testing design principles applied:**

- **Overconfidence traps (trick questions):** "Which weighs more: a ton of feathers or a ton of bricks?" (answer: same), "How many time zones does China officially use?" (answer: 1)
- **Underconfidence probes:** "What percentage of the ocean floor has been mapped?" (about 25% — most underestimate), "Approximately how many neurons are in the adult human brain?" (86 billion — sounds too high to believe)
- **Second-guess triggers:** Dunning-Kruger effect question, devil's interval in music, Mount Chimborazo vs Everest

### Step 4: Fixed 3 Pre-existing Test Failures

Three test files had been failing since before Sub-Task 6 due to Jest/Vitest incompatibilities:

**Fix 1: `tests/advancedAnalyticsStore.test.ts`** — Converted `jest.mock()` / `jest.fn()` / `jest.Mock` / `jest.clearAllMocks()` to Vitest equivalents (`vi.mock()` / `vi.fn()` / `vi.mocked()` / `vi.clearAllMocks()`).

**Fix 2: `tests/simulationStore.test.ts`** — Same Jest-to-Vitest conversion.

**Fix 3: `tests/scoringModule.test.ts`** — Replaced CommonJS `require('../src/utils/random').SeededRandom` with ES module `import { SeededRandom } from '../src/utils/random'` at top of file.

**Fix 4: `tests/advancedAnalyticsStore.mock.ts` + `src/store/advancedAnalyticsStore.ts`** — Fixed a latent bug in all three fallback Promise constructors where `setTimeout` callbacks could throw but the Promise only had `resolve` (no `reject`). Thrown errors became uncaught exceptions and the Promise never settled, causing 5-second timeouts. Added `reject` parameter and wrapped setTimeout bodies in try/catch.
